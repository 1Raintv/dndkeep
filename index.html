The D&D companion that automates the rules

DNDKeep is a free D&D 5e session companion for players and dungeon masters. Build characters with the 2024 Player's Handbook rules, track hit points and spell slots, roll dice with animated results, and run campaigns with real-time party sync — all in one place.

No spreadsheets. No manual math. When you apply a condition, the rules change automatically. When you roll, the result is instant.

Features

Guided character creator

Six-step wizard covering species, class, background, ability scores, and build choices. Supports all 13 classes, 14 species, and 23 backgrounds from the 2024 PHB.

Live character sheet

HP tracking, spell slots, conditions, weapons, inventory, saving throws, and skills — all automatically calculated. Click any ability score to roll it.

Conditions auto-apply

Poisoned means disadvantage on attack rolls. Stunned drops concentration. The rules apply themselves so you can focus on playing.

Dice with real results

Every roll triggers an animated result. Natural 20s glow gold. Fumbles flash red. Full modifier breakdown shown for every roll.

Spell browser

Search and filter 282 spells by level, school, and name. Add spells to your character and cast them directly from the sheet.

Campaign & DM tools

DMs create campaigns and share a join code. Real-time HP sync, initiative tracker, NPC attacks, and party view — all live at the table.
